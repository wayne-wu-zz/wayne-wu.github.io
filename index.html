About Me


Every art tells a story that connects to our feelings. Therefore, a world without art is essentialy a world without a heart. It is exactly the stories that I've come across growing up that have solidified my values, and the dream to create my own stories that can one day inspire someone else.

The forms of art are constantly evolving thanks to the tech industry that continues to follow Moore's Law. Technology powers art by delivering the stories through our human senses. As such, I aspire to learn the science behind these innovations, and ultimately create technologies that elevate further our ability to preceive arts.
