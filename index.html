About Me

Art, Technology, and Alcohol. Those are the three things that define me as a person.

Every art tells a story that connects to our feelings. Therefore, a world without art is essentialy a world without a heart. It is exactly the stories that I've come across growing up that have solidified my values, and the dream to create my own stories that can inspire someone else.

The forms of art are constantly evolving thanks to technology that continues to follow Moore's Law. Technology powers art by delivering the stories through our human senses. As such, there is no better place for me than in engineering to learn the science and theories behind these innovations, and ultimately create products that elevate further our ability to preceive arts.

Finally, alcohol connects people, the creators of art and technology. A glass of wine holds two hands together. A pint of beer echoes the laughters of friends. That's why I choose to be a bartender when I'm not learning about art or technology, to connect with people and to connect people together.

Wayne Wu
